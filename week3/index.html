Site Name

Suomi Rapids - A race to the Finnish line!

Site Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the website is to advertise my rafting company. Excite the user, how amazing the experience would be. Essentially convince the user that this location and with my boats and possible raft guiders would be the best possible way to go. Entice the user that is is very affordable and a new experience that they would find. Make navigating and scheduling times on the website would be very important.

Target Audience

Target audience would be college age students and families. College age students would be a great audience, most likely on a mobile device. They would want to know how soon in advance they can schedule as they're not likely to plan ahead. Group package options for both families as well as college students as they will be glad to take any discount for themselves as students or if they come in large groups. Available lodging and parking nearby are on some other rafting websites I noticed. Also what to expect, as in the difficulty of the rapids. For the avid rapid rafters you will want to tell them if these are more calm and family friendly or not for the faint of heart.
